Take Your Taste Buds on a Mediterranean Adventure with these Veggie Dishes from Italy and Beyond

  • 21 December 2017
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 3 min
veggie vegetarian mediterranean spain italy greece world food cuisine singapore
Share this article

We all love to eat great food and the food from the Mediterranean is popular worldwide. Countries like Italy, Spain, and Greece are known for their amazing cuisines and fantastic flavours. It's not just the meat dishes that are the best either; there are so many incredible vegetable dishes out there that are just as good if not better. So, let's dive in and explore the best veggie foods straight from the Med.

1. Trofie alla Genovise - Riccioti Riverwalk

For a filling and hearty pasta dish that will warm your stomach, make sure you grab a bowl of the Trofie all Genovise made by the guys at Riccioti Riverwalk. This meat-free meal is the very definition of eating your greens as everything on the plate is green! Riccioti Riverwalk only serves the best Italian food, where each dish gives you an authentic taste of Italy. This dish is made with trofie short pasta, basil pesto, green beans, and potatoes and is so filling and tasty you'll be contented for hours after eating.

A post shared by Drool Cook Club (@droolcookclub) on May 21, 2016 at 12:07am PDT

2. Ravioli Ricotta and Spinach - Cibo Italiano

Ravioli is another classic Italian dish that can be found in Mediterranean restaurants all around the world, and some of the best dishes in the city are the Ravioli Ricotta and Spinach served at Cibo Italiano. This dish is made with homemade egg pasta, which is filled with Italian ricotta and served with spinach in tomato sauce and basil. All of the ingredients are totally fresh and you can taste it with every bite.

A post shared by Momo (@made_by_momo_) on Apr 20, 2017 at 5:37am PDT

3. Greek Lasagne - ERGON Greek Deli and Café

It's not just the Italians who should get all the credit for making great lasagne - the Greeks have made the dish their own as well. The Greek Lasagne served at ERGON Greek Deli and Café is simple yet effective, made with spinach lasagne with feta cheese and served with a fresh and crispy Greek salad. It is a great option for lunch or dinner and, like the rest of the food served at ERGON, tastes amazing every time.

A post shared by Claudia (@claudocy) on Nov 13, 2017 at 7:55am PST

4. Forest Mushroom and Zucchini Pasta - Etto

Etto is a Spanish restaurant that showcases some of the best Spanish dishes and their Forest Mushroom and Zucchini Pasta is no exception. It is a deliciously fresh and filling dish made simply with wild mushrooms, zucchini, and linguine, which is bursting with flavour and is sure to become a regular order once you try it.

These veggie Mediterranean dishes taste amazing and don't need a hint of meat to make them any better so make it your mission to try these dishes out and experience some amazing meals.
