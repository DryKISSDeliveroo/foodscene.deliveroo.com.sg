Rise and shine – the best romantic breakfasts for two

  • 21 November 2017
  • Vanessa Teo
  • Read time: 3 min
romantic breakfasts european asian breakfast singapore brunch dessert
Share this article

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, and it is made even better when you get to share it with a loved one. There's nothing quite like tucking into a scrumptious breakfast or brunch with your significant other to start the day properly, so let's explore some of the best breakfasts in Singapore you can enjoy with your loved one.

1. Homemade English Breakfast

If there's a nation who knows how to do breakfast, it's the English. Their famous Full English Breakfasts are the perfect way to start the day, and the ones served over at d'Good Café are some of the best in town. They consist of sausages, baked beans, fried tomatoes, fried eggs, bacon, mushrooms and buttery toast, and are a great treat for any day of the week. What's more, the Full English at d'Good Café is served all day long, so even if you and your loved one are late sleepers you can still get in on the yummy goodness.

Where: d'Good Café, Holland Avenue, Singapore.

A post shared by d'Good Cafe (@dgoodcafe) on Sep 11, 2015 at 5:11pm PDT

2. Minced Meat Omelette

If you're a couple who enjoys a savoury breakfast, then be sure to try out the Minced Meat Omelette served over at Feng Ji Chicken. It's a dish that can be enjoyed at any time of the day so why not make it your first meal of the day? It's a proper taste of a Chinese-style breakfast and if you or your loved one haven't tried it before then it's a great food adventure to embark on together. You can be sure it'll keep hunger locked away till lunch!

Where: Feng Ji Chicken, River Valley Road, Singapore.

3. Summer Garden Super Bowl

If you're looking to get onto a couple's health kick or just want the healthiest breakfast possible, then be sure to get your hands on the Summer Garden Super Bowl over at Craftsmen. It is a big bowl full of green goodness, such as mixed mesclun greens, fresh pomegranate, beetroot, cherry tomatoes, feta, roasted nuts, sautéed mushroom with balsamic dressing and not one, not two, but three single portions of pulled pork to top it all off. It's probably big enough to share with your partner, but don't feel bad about getting your own all to yourself, they're delicious!

Where: Craftsmen, First Street, Singapore

A post shared by Eu Gine Soh Lim (@euginemay) on Nov 21, 2017 at 2:07am PST

4. Pancakes with Warm Maple Butter

When it comes to breakfast, it's safe to say that pancakes are king. Whether you enjoy them sweet or savoury, there is something for everyone's tastes. The Pancakes with Warm Maple Butter served at Clinton Street Baking Co. & Kitchen are truly delectable, and you can choose from toppings such as blueberries, banana walnut, or chocolate chunks. You could even get all of the toppings and share out the pancakes together!

Where: Clinton Street Baking Co & Kitchen, Purvis Street, Singapore.

A post shared by Clinton Street Baking SG (@clintonst_sg) on Oct 5, 2017 at 6:43pm PDT
