How do you like your eggs in the morning? 4 brilliant breakfast ideas

  • 27 March 2017
  • Editor
  • Read time: 3 min
breakfast smoked salmon avocado egg
Share this article

It's never been easier to make breakfast special. From sensationally sweet treats to fried masterpieces, there really is something for everyone. Here's a quick round-up of some of our favourite breakfasts in Singapore.

1. Avocado Egg Open Face, Artisan Boulangerie Co.

A post shared by Artisan Boulangerie Co. (@artisanboulangerie) on Jan 4, 2017 at 4:49am PST

Avocado is fast becoming a very popular choice for morning meals. Artisan Boulangerie Co. jumped on this trend early, and now their Avocado Egg Open Face has become an ideal option to pair with your first coffee of the day.

Wholewheat toast is topped with enough scrambled eggs to fill even the hungriest of diners. Add on lightly grilled fresh tomatoes, mushrooms, crispy bacon and diced avocado and you've got Boulangerie's trendiest morning dish. Plus, it'll keep you full all day long.
