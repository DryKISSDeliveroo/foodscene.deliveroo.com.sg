Oh for the love of meat

  • 16 March 2018
  • Mark Cheong
  • Read time: 5 min
meat festival of feasts carnivore carnival steak chicken wings
Share this article

From steaks to chicken wings, nothing's quite as satisfying as a hefty bite into some juicy meats - that's why we're celebrating the Carnivore Carnival for the Festival of Feasts. 1-for-1 deals, $12 meals, bundle sets, tasting platters on all things meat.

To get you prepped for the meat mania, here's our guide to all things meat on Deliveroo.

Wings, glorious wings

Remember Wing-It Wednesday? Seems we haven't quite satisfied our love for juicy chicken wings. Be it barbecued, fried, grilled, seasoned in parmesan, Singapore has no lack of chicken wings for carnivores to feast on.

Enter Outback Steakhouse's Kookaburra Wings. Named after Australia's national bird (that sits on the old gum tree), these distinctively Aussie wings are tossed in Outback Steakhouse's secret spices and served with blue cheese dressing and celery. If you're hankering for a spicy treat, or think you're more in the mood for a milder ride, you've got the choice to have it served mild, medium, or hot.

This week at the Carnivore Carnival, these wings are going for 1-for-1 this week, for all your chicken wing cravings. Get them from Outback Steakhouse - Orchard Gateway!

Burger-licious

We all have this never-ending fascination for what we can come up with in a burger or sandwich. It's a simple formula - meat, 2 buns, sauce, endless possibilities. All in a (mostly) manageable package that fits perfectly in your hands. It's clear, burgers were a gift to mankind.

So imagine our faces when Meat Smith Little India came in with their Hot Delhi Chicken Sandwich (which you can get 1-for-1 for this week). A crispy chicken patty coated in Indian spices, and house dill pickles sitting comfortably in a house brioche bun. We salivated, we drooled, we put our hands together, and ate it. And it was good.

As part of the Carnivore Carnival, you too can get Meat Smith Little India's Hot Delhi Chicken Sandwich in a 1-for-1 deal.

You know what's at steak

And now we've come to what everyone thinks about the moment the word "beef" is uttered. From ribeyes to sirloin cuts, rare or well done, we know what's at steak here. We all have opinions on how steak should be made, and the battle lines are drawn. Some would say that you should only have steak medium or medium rare, some would prefer ribeye cuts, some swear by having their steak well done. Whichever side of the fence you are on, we can all agree that a good steak really hits the spot.

L'Entrecôte The Steak & Fries Bistro lives up to its name here, focusing on getting the best steak every season, and pairing it with crispy golden fries and fresh green salad with mustard dressing and walnuts. With the Festival of Feasts, pair that with a Pâté de Campagne, Salade d'Endives aux Noix et Bleu or Leek & Vinaigrette and a Signature Lemon Tart. All this to create the perfect bundle set for any meal lover.

BBQ Basket

Sometimes, celebrating meat means getting down and dirty with a good old fashioned barbeque. There's a great satisfaction in cooking your own steak, or getting the chicken wings seared just right. Down at Deliveroo Editions Katong, we've worked with The Meat Club to bring you BBQ Basket by Editions to bring you all your meaty needs for the weekend barbie.

For the Carnivore Carnival, we've got BBQ Baskets for 4, 8, 12, or 20 people at lower prices with your own choice of meats to choose from. To complete the experience, get yourself some dips from our other Deliveroo Editions restaurants, and great beer from the Brussels Beer Project!

Next week: No grain, no gain

The Festival of Feasts doesn't end here! Next week we're celebrating everything to do with grain with 'No Grain, No Gain'. Pastas, pizzas, grain bowls, pastries… Look out for deals from Tiong Bahru Bakery, Grain Traders, Spizza, plus some secret deals from your favourite local delights. Get ready to get aboard the grain train on Deliveroo.
