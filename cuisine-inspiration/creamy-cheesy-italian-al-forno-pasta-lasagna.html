Creamy, Cheesy And Indulgent – Go Al Forno Next Time You Order Italian

  • 16 February 2018
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 3 min
cheesy italian pasta lasagna carbonara pizza
Share this article

Few meals compare to the ultimate comfort food that is the creamy, cheesy delights exclusive to authentic Italian dishes – especially those baked "al forno" to get that extra special molten-effect in your cheese. Whether you're craving a piping hot, freshly-baked lasagna, decadent pasta or cheesy pizza, you can be sure all of these fantastic Italian meals will deliver wholesome satisfaction. Ready to dive right in? Check out our list of the best cheese-slathered dishes guaranteed to please the whole family right here in Singapore!

1. The Parma Pizza - Jamie's Italian

A post shared by Jamie's Italian Singapore (@jamiesitaliansg) on Nov 26, 2016 at 5:55pm PST

First up on our list of cheesy goodness from Italy is the Parma Pizza from Jamie's Italian. A pizza dripping with flavour and incredible aromas, the Parma is more than equipped to satisfy those cheesy cravings you just can't deny. A perfectly-baked doughy base is coated in fresh homemade tomato sauce, San Daniele Italian prosciutto, garden-fresh rocket and of course, a heaping mixture of mozzarella and shaved parmesan for that ultimate bubbling layer you're looking for. Order in a slice of the Parma with a glass of wine and your evening is set!

2. Trio Lasagne - Super Dario

A post shared by Andrew (@andrew.sg) on Mar 4, 2017 at 3:51am PST

Priding themselves on their incredible variety of freshly-baked lasagne recipes at Super Dario, you can rest easy knowing all your rich and creamy favourites are ready on demand the next time you feel a rumble coming on. If you're keen for a veggie-fuelled feast, the Trio from Super Dario is a captivating vegetarian lasagne packed to the brim with fresh ingredients and of course, cheese. Lots and lots of cheese! Fresh zucchini and sweet bell peppers are infused into this lasagne with a heavily indulgent scamorza, a delicious smoked cheese that completes this magical al forno dish.

3. Portobello Vegetarian - Limoncello

Looking for something a little different that still offers that all-time favourite creamy touch? Feast your eyes on yet another exquisite Italian baked marvel, the Vegetarian Portobello from Limoncello. A wholesome Portobello mushroom is oven baked to perfection and dressed with a savoury mountain of sautéed spinach before being generously drizzled with a skillfully-prepared parmesan cheese cream sauce. A perfect baked alternative to other, more traditional al forno dishes, with the Vegetarian Portobello you can enjoy a guiltless treat and still get that creamy delight your heart desires. Or, seeing as it's a starter, you can have it as a light prelude to a gorge-ful Italian mean. Either way, this one is guaranteed to make your list of favourites.

4. Spaghetti Carbonara - Al Forno

Last but not least on our guide to the creamiest Italian favourites in town, the Spaghetti Carbonara is a timeless classic from the appropriate-named Al Forno. Internationally loved, carbonara is a marvellous cheese-infused spaghetti dish featuring a full spectrum of delicious flavours and creamy goodness. Authentic Italian spices are blended with spaghetti, crisp bacon shavings, cream, eggs and a heavenly topping of parmesan cheese – a combination truly made with dairy-lovers in mind.

Indulge your senses in any of these deliciously creamy al forno dishes with the help of Deliveroo!
