Savoury snacks from heaven - from the classic curry puff to chicken wings, we're loving these light bites!

  • 21 June 2018
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 3 min
bao curry chicken potatoes spice chicken wings korean chinese
Share this article

Snacking isn't just for those with a sweet tooth - there are plenty of savoury options as well. Across all kinds of different cuisine from Chinese to Indian, you'll find something addictive that will work for you. On Deliveroo, you won't be short of options, from a crispy curry puff to baos that "wow" instead. Each of the following delicious dishes are hand-picked because we know full well that once you try them, you won't be able to live without them. "Sorry, not sorry" in advance!

1. Curry Puffs

If you love South East Asian food, curry puffs are something of a dream. Typically filled with chicken or potatoes - or a combination of both - they're crispy on the outside and yet fluffy within. Tip Top gives us everything we're looking for when it comes to the curry puff - their Chicken Curry Puff is loaded with juicy chicken chunks, premium potatoes and egg. And that's not all. The real secret to their success is in their signature blend of spices. Truly the best!

2. Kouign Amann Cake

Is it savoury? Is it sweet? We're going to say that the kouign mann cake is somewhere in between. It's made of bread dough with layers of butter and sugar folded in. Hailing from France, it's every bit as moreish as you might imagine, so you won't just want one piece. In recent years it's gained even more popularity and it's popping up all over the place, thanks to the ever-amazing "Great British Bake Off" in the UK. That influence has found its way all the way over to Tiong Bahru Bakery, where the Kouign Amann is a signature staple. We recommend trying one if you're late to the party - stat. 

A post shared by Tiong Bahru Bakery (@tiongbahrubakery) on Nov 9, 2017 at 10:29pm PST

3. Baos

We love baos of every sort - and there are a lot of them, so that's saying something! We're obsessed with them all and everything about them, from the dumpling's cute little size to their fillings like veggies and meat. Throughout China, they've been enjoyed for literally centuries and these days you can enjoy them in Singapore. Crystal Jade La Mian Xiao Long Bao's got a big name but an even bigger (and better) menu. Fans of dim sum will enjoy 101 Steamed Shanghai Xiao Long Bao. These parcels are perfect and traditionally filled with pork. You get four pieces: enough to savour each one.

A post shared by Gerard (@gerardtx_) on Sep 27, 2015 at 9:21am PDT

4. Fried Chicken Wings

When you order chicken wings, it's impossible to just eat one - you'll find yourself eating as many as you can manage - and then some. Test yourself at Oven Fried Chicken by ordering the Fried Wings Half or really rise to the challenge with the Fried Wings Whole. Whichever portion you choose, you've got succulent, fresh and locally sourced Korean-style fried chicken wings - and you won't regret it. Those proving a point with the full whack have twenty wings in store. Yes, twenty. You could share, but that's just nowhere near as tasty. Go on, you can do it!

A post shared by Justin Teo (@justinfoodprints) on Jan 6, 2016 at 2:24am PST
