Going Loco In Singapore - Where To Grab The Best Mexican Food

  • 12 January 2018
  • Vanessa Teo
  • Read time: 4 min
mexican tacos burritos churros singapore
Share this article

If you can't resist fajitas and absolutely love tacos then perhaps it is time to go loco in Singapore. The number of amazing Mexican restaurants around here means that you can choose from a great variety of traditional Latin American dishes that are sure to delight you. The following are some of the places where you can grab the best Mexican food that will put a smile on your face.

1.   Muchos - Plaza Singapura

A post shared by Eileen Lee (@eileenthewhatever) on Apr 4, 2015 at 1:18am PDT

You can find a little slice of Mexico tucked away in Plaza Singapura with Muchos restaurant. This popular spot has appetisers such as Chile Con Queso and Nachos Con Salsa to get you started in style. After that, the extensive selection of nibbles covers delights such as Grilled Corn, Breaded Fish Nuggets and Mexican Chicken Wings. As for the main courses on offer, you can choose from classics like Fajitas, Chili Con Carne and Burritos.

2.   Super Loco - Robertson Quay

A post shared by ❀ sarah ❀ (@purplediaries) on Apr 30, 2017 at 4:45am PDT

There is a great mixture of options to pick from throughout the day here at Super Loco, from the Breakfast Grande to lots of different main meals, drinks and tasty postres to round things off. The main dishes include spicy Mexican treats like Costillas de Cerdo, Tacos and Burritos. As for those delicious postres, Pastel de Maiz is a sweet-corn pudding with salted dulce de leche, and Super Churros are crispy churros with Mexican-style chocolate sauce.  

3.   Baja Fresh Mexican Grill - Marine Boulevard

A post shared by A Forever-Hungry Community (@burpple) on Apr 5, 2016 at 4:49am PDT

Pretty much all of the classic Mexican dishes you could crave are listed on the excellent menu at Baja Fresh Mexican Grill. From Burritos to Quesadillas and from a Taco Platter to Fajitas, there is something here for every taste. There are also a few different breakfast options; Americano Breakfast, Breakfast Burrito and our favourite, the Huevos Ranchero Burrito. Sweet-toothed diners can choose from the likes of Chocolate Muffin, Chocolate Walnut Brownie and Honey Yoghurt Granola Pot to finish the meal with a bang.

4.   Mex Out - Far East Square

A post shared by Mex Out (@mexout) on Nov 9, 2017 at 8:22pm PST

Mex Out on Far East Square is a place where you can try all sorts of different Mexican food in one go. Among the most popular dishes here are the Salad Bowl, the Rice Bowl and the Large Burrito Wrap. With the Tacos, this is where you get to choose your favourite type, from Baja Style, filled with tender steak, to Street Style varieties that are both extremely tempting. The Nachos are another great choice here, being loaded with guacamole and tomato salsa, while the Churros are unlikely to disappoint anyone who enjoys the sweeter side of life.

5.   Senor Taco - Clarke Quay

A post shared by Sue Lynn Tiong (@bangsarbabe) on Apr 4, 2015 at 6:03am PDT

If what you want are some tremendous, authentic Tacos then Senor Taco is a great spot to try. The different Taco dishes here include Fish Baja Style, Pork Belly and Vegetarian Chorizo. Don't let the restaurant name fool you though, there are plenty of other dishes to choose from ranging from 10 inch quesadillas, loaded burritos, cheesy enchiladas and sizzling fajitas all on offer also. You could also choose one of the dishes for sharing such as Chips & Salsa, Chicken Wings or Prawn Ceviche.

Why not take a look through the Mexican restaurants on Deliveroo the next time that you want to add some Mexican spice to your day?
