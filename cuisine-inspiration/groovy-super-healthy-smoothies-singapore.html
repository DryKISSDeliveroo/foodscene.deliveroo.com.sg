Singapore's healthiest super smoothies to grab for a quick lunch

  • 18 December 2017
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 4 min
healthy lunch smoothies vegetarian fruit vegetables drinks singapore
Share this article

Life can come at you fast sometimes which means that staying healthy and getting your five a day can be a bit of a struggle. This often means grabbing a quick bite to eat as we get on with our day which isn't always the healthiest option. But the popularity of healthy meal-replacement drinks means that smoothies are a great way to get your daily dose of fruit and veg and give a great little energy boost, as they are packed full of yummy treats and are easy to take on the go or just sit and savour. So, let's explore some of the best smoothies you can get your hands on in Singapore.

1. Incredible Hulk - Kitchen by Food Rebel

A post shared by Kitchen by Food Rebel (@foodrebelsg) on Feb 20, 2016 at 7:54pm PST

Food Rebel only believes in making the healthiest foods and drinks made with fresh and nutritious ingredients to give you everything you need for the day. This quality is maintained throughout everything they serve, right down to their smoothies. The Incredible Hulk is a drink designed to fill you up and energise you. It is made with organic vegetables, orange, banana, ginger and packed with protein powder to make the perfect meal replacement drink which is full of nutrients.

2. Immune Jab - Real Food

A post shared by Pamela Hannie (@mhelahoney) on Nov 1, 2017 at 5:32am PDT

Getting ill is the worst thing, it leaves you feeling dreadful and you get nothing productive done, so be sure to take preventative measures by trying out the Immune Jab from Real Food. Real Food is run by a team of people who only make the most nutritious foods free from any artificial preservatives, making it possibly the healthiest place in Singapore. This smoothie is packed with antioxidants and is 100% vegan-friendly. It is made with bananas, oranges, organic soy milk, soy yoghurt and chia seeds. The vitamin C helps to strengthen your immune system and prevent illness, and antioxidants help prevent the production of free radicals, so if you're looking to stay fit and healthy then you must try out one of these!

3. Breakfast Smoothie - Haakon Superfoods and Juice

A post shared by HAAKON Superfoods And Juice (@haakon.sg) on Nov 1, 2017 at 7:47pm PDT

Life in Singapore can happen very quickly with the fast pace of life, so for those mornings when you don't have the time to make breakfast, try out the Breakfast Smoothie over at Haakon Superfoods and Juice. This smoothie is made to give you all the essentials a proper breakfast should have, as it is packed with tasty almond butter, oats, soy, whey protein, banana, and probiotic yoghurt. It is a natural appetite suppressant so you don't need to worry about spending your morning with your stomach grumbling, and everything in it is healthy so you're doing your body a favour.

4. Hangover Healer - Shake Farm

A post shared by Shake Farm Singapore (@shakefarm) on Aug 25, 2017 at 1:00am PDT

We're all guilty of having a few too many drinks on a night out and waking up with a bad headache and generally feeling terrible. Well, not to worry, just pick yourself up a Hangover Healer from Shake Farm to repent for your night of debauchery and forget it ever happened! This smoothie is made with all natural ingredients including blueberries, banana, spinach, chia seeds, organic honey, cinnamon, coconut water and probiotic yoghurt to give you all of the stuff you need to feel strong and healthy again as well as keep you feeling full for hours.

Smoothies are a godsend for whether you're in a hurry or just looking for a healthy drink to enjoy. Get your hands on any of these smoothies and see what all the hype is about!
