Eat Your Way Healthy, Halal And Happy In Singapore

  • 13 January 2018
  • Vanessa Teo
  • Read time: 3 min
halal healthy indonesian indian korean
Share this article

There is no need to think that eating healthy halal food is going to difficult in Singapore or that you are going to have limited options. Check out these top 5 restaurants offering halal in Singapore that meets your needs and have nutritious, tempting menus also.

1.   Bebek Goreng Pak Ndut - Orchard Road

A post shared by MISSTAMCHIAK.com (@misstamchiak) on Aug 30, 2017 at 2:34am PDT

There are some great reasons for believing that you will get the top class Halal meal you want at Bebek Goreng Pak Ndut. This Indonesian restaurant has delicious treats such as Orange Crispy Fried Duck, Original Smash Fried Chicken and Crispy Fried Chicken in Green Chilli which are all served with a side of rice. Side dishes worth trying include the Fried Egg & Tofu and the Crispy Duck Feet, as well as the Fried Beef Ball. You will also find an interesting soup menu containing the likes of Duck Soup, Oxtail Soup, Chicken Soup, and Sour Soup.

2.   YOLO - Gopeng Street

A post shared by #EatYourGoals (@yolofoodsg) on Nov 8, 2017 at 6:43pm PST

With a wide choice of vegetarian and Halal dishes, YOLO is a fine choice for trying Asian fusion cuisine that can also help you on your healthy food journey. Among the highlights on their menu are the two Shape Up Signature Dishes. These are low-calorie goal-oriented meals that will help you with your fitness goals. Try the 300 calorie Cauliflower and "Fried" Rice with Chicken and Veggies, together with the 320 calorie Tomato Soup with Chicken, Avocado and Beans. Other Signature Dishes designed by their nutritionist are the 680 calorie Arnie's Meatballs with Pasta & Eggplants, as well as the 580 calorie Basil Minced Chicken with Brown Rice & Egg.

3.   IndoChili - Zion Road

A post shared by Diyanah Yahya (@deeyanahyahya) on Nov 22, 2015 at 5:45am PST

You won't be short of healthy choices and Halal dishes once you start looking through the menu at the Indonesian restaurant IndoChili. For instance, if you want a tasty starter to get mealtime going, then options such as Sate Ayam Madura and Sayur Asem are a couple worth taking a glance at. Moving down to the soups, Sop Buntut and Rawon Komplit are among the best ones available here. As for the main meals, you won't go wrong with the Ayam Panggang Padang, which is grilled chicken dipped in Padang style sauce.

4.   New Shah Alam - Circular Road

A post shared by Plong (@plongin) on Aug 19, 2015 at 2:49am PDT

New Shah Alam is an Indian Halal restaurant with an impressive variety of quality dishes. At the time of writing, the Specials for Lunch section has delights such as Chicken Dum Briyani on Wednesday and Mutton Dum Briyani on Thursday with the dish of the day for Friday being Fish Dum Briyani. You will also see a lovely range of Chicken Varieties, such as Chicken Masala, Mysore Chicken, and Chili Chicken. The Seafood section includes Fish Masala and Curry Fish. If you are hunting for Halal in Singapore then this is a place to bear in mind.

5.   NeNe Chicken - Orchard Link

A post shared by Nene Chicken Singapore (@nenechickensg) on Nov 27, 2017 at 8:02pm PST

The Korean Halal cuisine on offer at NeNe Chicken is an absolute delight, as well as being healthier than anything that tastes this good has any right to be. Heading up the menu is their famous bap burgers while chicken tenders, wings and sticks all equally vie for your attention. Side orders include curly fries, soy rice and spam fries.

Give the best healthy, Halal eating options in Singapore a try by looking up your favourite dishes on Deliveroo.
