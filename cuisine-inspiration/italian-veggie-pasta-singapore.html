Mamma mia, sono vegetariano - 5 tempting pasta dishes for veggies in Singapore

  • 27 December 2017
  • Vanessa Teo
  • Read time: 3 min
italian vegetarian pasta veggie arrabiata vegetarian lasagne
Share this article

Finding a pasta dish with no meat in it is now easier than ever before in Singapore. Some of the city's top chefs have worked wonders in rustling up menus that bring us highly creative veggie pasta dishes. Whether you want lasagne that is crammed with goodness, penne in a spicy sauce or something else, you can take your pick and enjoy a healthy Italian takeaway meal that you love.

1. Arrabiata - The Marmalade Pantry

A post shared by The Marmalade Pantry (@themarmaladepantry) on May 16, 2016 at 3:05am PDT

If you want something spicy and fiery then the Arrabiata from The Marmalade Pantry will set your taste buds tingling. This is a combination of penne pasta with grilled aubergine and feta cheese, served with a spicy tomato sauce that will add a real touch of pleasure to your meal-time. This restaurant also delivers some magnificently indulgent desserts to top it off. With the likes of Old Fashioned Chocolate Fudge and Sea Salt Caramel Cheesecake, you are completely spoiled for choice here.

2. Spaghetti Aglio Olio e Funghi - Da Paolo HQ Club Street

A post shared by Wanyu 🌈🦄 (@wanyukoo) on Oct 8, 2014 at 9:58pm PDT

If you want pasta served with a velvety, creamy tomato sauce then Da Paolo HQ Club Street is one of your best options in Singapore. Among their extensive range of dishes, the delightful Spaghetti Aglio Olio e Funghi stands out as being especially tempting. This is a wonderful mixture of flavours that sees mushrooms sautéed in garlic, chilli and extra virgin olive oil. They also offer an exciting range of pizzas with a variety of vegetarian toppings that you might be interested in too.

3. Roasted Veggie Lasagne - Alt.Pizza

A post shared by Alt. Pizza (@altpizza) on Dec 8, 2016 at 5:59pm PST

Finding a tasty veggie lasagne in Singapore is always great news. While Alt. Pizza is best known for its thrilling range of pizzas, it also does a terrific lasagne. This pasta dish is stuffed full of roasted vegetables, together with béchamel sauce, Napoli sauce, pesto, parmesan and herbs. Top off your meal with their famous Chocolate Lava Cake and you are going to have a great time. If you are tempted by a veggie pizza then their Hipster and Lean & Green pizzas are well worth taking a look at too.

4. Green Garden - P.S. Café

 

A post shared by PS.Cafe (@pscafe) on Dec 1, 2017 at 1:06am PST

This pasta dish is a real treat for vegetarians in Singapore looking for as healthy a choice as possible, as it brings together wholewheat penne and a variety of green garden vegetables. It is served with homemade herb pine nut pistou and looks as good as it tastes. The popular P.S. Café is also a fine place to get a salad such as their Garden Salad, Superfood Salad or Big Greek.

5. Ravioli - Spizza

A post shared by Spizza (@spizzasg) on Jun 2, 2015 at 9:54pm PDT

Another Singapore restaurant that does a great line of pizzas and pasta, Spizza has a number of interesting veggie dishes that are well worth exploring. Among them is a classic Ravioli that is filled with ricotta and spinach. Served with a tomato sauce, this is a little piece of Italy that you won't regret ordering. Among the other dishes on their menu are plenty of pizzas, pasta and a very nice Funghi soup.

When you crave some pasta, then just head over to Deliveroo and find out how many appetising veggie options are waiting for you.
