Our lowest prices (so far)...

  • 30 January 2018
  • Mark Cheong
  • Read time: 5 min
lunch dinner cheap bowl western sushi soup the daily cut collin's maki-san the soup spoon
Share this article

Getting great food with Deliveroo is now as easy as 1, 2, 3. We've lowered our minimum spend to just $12 and our delivery fee to $3! We've heard what you've been looking for, and these are the lowest our minimum spend and delivery fee have been, and we're not looking back.

Lunch or dinner, breakfast or dessert. Everyone deserves some awesome food. As 2018 is in full swing, we'll always be looking to improve our selection of restaurants with the same great service you've come to know and love.

Afternoon Delights.

Ever needed that mid-afternoon pick me up? Thinking of dining al desko and treating yourself for an office table lunch? Or perhaps you're feeling generous and want to treat friends and colleagues to a 3pm treat. Whatever it is, lower delivery fees means no more holding back on that afternoon snack or office treats.

The Daily Cut

The Daily Cut has been feeding hungry hungry office folks from 2014, and with good reason. Their bowls are chock full with ingredients aren't just delicious. At $12 for a regular bowl, they're great value for money as well, and just nice for our lower minimum spend!  (Check it out, their Chicken Salad Bowl is literally the 8th best selling dish on Deliveroo worldwide!)

If you can't decide what to add to your bowl, we suggest giving the rib-eye steak a shot. Juicy steak strips in your salad help to balance the veggies for an all-round perfect lunch crowd.

Take advantage of our lower delivery fee and minimum spend by getting a bowl from The Daily Cut.

Maki-San

Where The Daily Cut lets you design your own bowl, Maki-San gives you the power to design your own sushi, at a reasonable price ($9.90 for a Mega-San with 5 main ingredients!). Your wish is their command. The list of ingredients you can get in your sushi is almost endless, with anything from tuna mayo to a more exotic and unfamiliar taste of sambal and ikan bilis.

While you can take your time to order and decide to your heart's content, you may need someone to help make the decisions for you if you're in a hurry. In that case, try one of their house sushi flavours. The California Dreaming proves to be a house favourite.

Get some sushi from Maki-San with our lower delivery fee and minimum spend.

Dinner Dates

Our minimum spend for dinner has been lowered from $18 to $12! That means more dinner desserts heading your way. It means late nights in the office won't be so lonely anymore. It means treating yourself to a great dinner, whenever you want.

COLLIN'S®

Nothing beats the nostalgic flavours of our favourite western dishes as we remembered them. That's why COLLIN'S® has been such a hit with us. Great western favourites, at amazing prices, ripe for the taking given our lower minimum spend.

One of our favourites from COLLIN'S® has to be one of their Hot Kitchen Entree, their Grilled Large Prawns served with Mexican Pesto Sauce and a side a Pasta, Corn, and Salad, at the great price of $11.80! Just add a side of fries or a drink and you're good to go.

The Soup Spoon

The heat may be back, but sometimes all we want is to curl up with a warm bowl of soup in the evening after a long day and put on that series that our friends have been trying to get us to watch so that they can finally sidestep the spoiler warnings. (We all have that one friend who HASN'T watched Game of Thrones.)

That's where The Soup Spoon comes in. Warm, hearty soups and stews for those lunches and dinners. The feeling of home isn't far away. Try out some of their classic flavours from the crowd favourite Tokyo Chicken Stew with its refreshing clear soup, to the deeper thicker textures of their Beef Goulash. Pair it with a sandwich for a sandwich set at just $12.90.

Grab a bowl from The Soup Spoon on Deliveroo, with our lower minimum spend and delivery fee!
