Off the Grill and onto the Plate - Meaty Dishes to Chow Down On

  • 15 December 2017
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 3 min
off-the-grill-and-on-the-plate-best-meaty-dishes-dubai
Share this article

For meat lovers worldwide, there is nothing better than a proper high-quality cut of meat to chow down on for a really tasty and filling meal. It's, hearty, it's delicious, what's not to love? Dubai has some of the best meat dishes around, so let's look at some to get some inspiration for our next mealtime.

1. Pork Ribs BBQ Sauce - Meat Smith

There's nothing like getting your hands messy with a quality rack of ribs dripping with barbecue sauce made in an authentic steakhouse, which is exactly what the guys over at Meat Smith have aimed for with their Pork Ribs BBQ Sauce dish. These six-hour smoked pork ribs are dripping with delicious house-made barbecue sauce and are served with a side of pickles to top it all off. If you're looking for a juicy, meaty meal then you will not be disappointed. Just make sure you're hungry or you'll struggle to finish it all!

A post shared by Meatsmith SG (@meatsmith_sg) on Nov 16, 2017 at 8:44pm PST

2. Ribeye - Outback Steakhouse

If you're a steak lover looking for the best steak in town, then look no further. The Ribeye served at Outback Steakhouse is a steak-lover's dream. It is a savoury and juicy ribeye steak which is perfectly seasoned with a blend of seventeen (yes, seventeen) herbs and chargrilled over an open flame to achieve the perfect taste. This is 10oz of pure meaty goodness, so if you fancy yourself as a steak connoisseur or just a lover of great foods, then make sure you don't miss out on this.  

A post shared by Outback Steakhouse (@outback) on Sep 22, 2017 at 12:54pm PDT

3. Premium NZ Lamb Rack - New Ubin Seafood

For a meal that you will keep returning to, try out the Premium NZ Lamb Rack from New Ubin Seafood. This lamb rack comes in two generous pieces and is served with a delicious homemade mint sauce and sprinkled with sea salt flakes to bring out even more of the flavour of the meat. It is the perfect example of a high-quality rack of lamb and some of the best in all of Singapore, so make sure you try it out!

A post shared by MISSTAMCHIAK.com (@misstamchiak) on Oct 7, 2017 at 9:13pm PDT

4. Lucille - Kenny Rogers' Roasters

If you're hungry and looking for a big meal to really hit the spot then look no further than the Lucille served at Kenny Rogers' Roasters. This monster of a meal is made with half a rotisserie chicken as well as half a slab of baby back ribs and your choice of not one, not two but three regular sides. After this meal, you'll be forgetting what being hungry ever felt like in the first place!

A post shared by Kenny Rogers Roasters SG (@kennyrogers.sg) on Sep 3, 2017 at 3:45am PDT

There you have it, some of the best meaty dishes in all of Singapore. The next time you're craving a succulent, juicy piece of meat, then be sure to try out these dishes and you won't be disappointed.
