No meat means no worries with these vegan fast food dishes

  • 10 June 2018
  • Vanessa Teo
  • Read time: 4 min
vegan vegetarian fast food american burgers pizza dessert mexican
Share this article

From burgers to burritos, every now and then we like to treat ourselves to some tasty fast food dishes. It may not always be the healthiest option, but it's definitely a delicious one. It can be hard to find variety when it comes to vegan fast food, but it certainly isn't impossible. Singapore is home to a lot of great food joints and vegans needn't feel they're missing out on the action. From pizza to burgers to cakes galore, there are so many meat- and dairy-free dishes just bursting with flavour for you to try today.

1. Vegan Burger

A post shared by Sam W (@ditzykittys) on May 1, 2018 at 11:16pm PDT

The reigning king of the fast food empire has got to be the almighty burger. The guys over at Veganburg serve up some of the highest-quality vegan burgers in town and there's so much variety and flavour. From Avocado Beetroot to Char-Grilled Satay, there's something to impress no matter what your tastes. Their Smoky BBQ Burger is sure to blow your mind: made with a patty of oven-grilled minced mushrooms, covered with a barbecue sauce and layered with tomatoes, lettuce and onions, it's all sandwiched between a soft wholemeal bun. It's so good you'll wonder why anyone would ever eat a meaty burger! 

2. Vegan Pizza

A post shared by Reavathiy Varatharaja (@reavathiy) on Jan 26, 2017 at 1:58am PST

If you're craving a good pizza to enjoy while binge-watching your favourite TV show, you're not alone. Finding a decent slice of pizza that doesn't have cheese or meat on it isn't always easy, but Peace Cafe has come to the rescue with their range of vegan cheese pizzas. There's something for everyone here and their Sambal Vegan Pizza is definitely one to try out. We may not all agree with pineapple on pizza, but it totally works for this dish: the fresh pineapple is the perfect match for marinated tofu and the spicy sambal chilli base. Topped with a tasty vegan cheese, this pizza is sure to become your new favourite and you won't want to share a single slice!

3. Vegan Burrito

A post shared by Yosua Iskandar (@yosuaisk) on Jun 3, 2017 at 10:23pm PDT

Mexican food is delicious, but it can be quite meat-heavy. Not to worry, Baja Mexican Grill serves up some tasty, authentic burritos which are totally vegan-friendly. Their signature Mexican Burrito is a simple but tasty classic burrito wrap. It's got rice and beans along with your choice of veg for a filling and delicious meal. Eat it on your lunch break for an indulgent and filling lunch or save your order 'til later and look forward to it all day long

4. Vegan Cheesecake

A post shared by Eleanor (@vegan_dream_world) on Jul 18, 2016 at 5:05am PDT

No matter how full you are from dinner there's always room for a sweet treat and being vegan needn't reduce your cake options. Delcie's Desserts and Cakes specialise in vegan-friendly desserts and they're just as good, if not better, than their dairy counterparts. Their Red Velvet Cream Cheesecake is an absolute must-try. Its German chocolate sponge cake is topped with a delicious vegan cream cheese filling for an impressive take on the classic American dessert. Whether enjoying it with an afternoon cup of coffee or after a delicious meal, this cake will always leave a good impression.

The proof is in the pudding - order some vegan fast food to your door with Deliveroo and be astounded by the variety on offer.
