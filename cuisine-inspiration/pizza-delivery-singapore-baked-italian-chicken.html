Winner, Winner, Chicken Dinner! Chicken Pizza Delivery In Singapore

  • 19 February 2018
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 3 min
pizza italian napoli oven baked chicken cheese party
Share this article

Whether you're dining with friends or craving some late night pizza delivery in Singapore, we've got your back! The next time you feel the munchies coming on, check out this list to explore some of the most divine chicken pizzas in town.

1. Cajun Chicken - Crust Gourmet Pizza

A post shared by Crust Gourmet Pizza Bar (@crust_pizza) on Dec 11, 2017 at 10:32pm PST

Starting off strong and spicy, the Cajun Chicken from Crust Gourmet Pizza is not for the faint of heart. Cooking up some of the best-tasting pizza in town, Crust Gourmet guarantees every pizza will blow your mind and keep you ordering up seconds. Crisp Spanish onions are topped over a freshly made doughy base stacked with roasted peppers, mushrooms and of course, flaming jalapenos on Cajun Chicken. For an extra challenge, this spice-lovers' pizza is doused in peri-peri sauce and sizzling hot chilli to add even more feistiness to every bite. A definite must-try for the champions in town, be sure to get your hands on your very own slice today!

2. BBQ Chicken Pizza - Napolizz Pizza

A post shared by HA (@hateedumpty) on Aug 1, 2014 at 9:25am PDT

A thin-crust pizza designed to satisfy even the most dedicated of pizza-lovers, every slice of out Napolizz's kitchen is aimed at delivering an authentic and memorable experience. The BBQ chicken pizza - a modern classic - is just as good as it sounds. Melted mozzarella and fresh tomato sauce are generously layered atop a freshly baked thin crust and drizzled with spicy honey BBQ chicken pieces to produce a world-class favourite. Throw in some red onions and more cheese and you've got yourself a perfect pizza for the perfect night in from Napolizz.

3. Truffle Shuffle - Alt. Pizza

A post shared by Alt. Pizza (@altpizza) on Feb 1, 2018 at 8:06pm PST

When it comes to exquisite gourmet pizza, check out Alt. Pizza's Truffle Shuffle for something a little more on the lux side. The Truffle Shuffle features a creamy white béchamel sauce spread over juicy chicken fennel sausages and roasted mushrooms. An enticing truffle oil dressing adds a boost of aroma, and a gooey blanket of melted parmesan and mozzarella cheese tops it all off. Need we say more? As beautiful as it sounds, the Truffle Shuffle offers a whole new level of satisfaction when it comes to chicken pizza and proudly boasts its rightfully-earned place among Singapore's best.

4. Buffalo Chicken Pizza - The Manhattan Pizza Co

A post shared by Stella(雪琪) (@stxq) on Mar 3, 2014 at 2:31am PST

Keeping true to the traditional Neapolitan pizza, The Manhattan Pizza Co serves up some incredibly delicious (and massive) slices of authentic Italian flavour. The Buffalo Chicken Pizza is a marvel in its own right, consisting of a fresh marinara sauce topped with the juiciest grilled chicken, shredded mozzarella, crisp spring onions and delectable shallots. If that wasn't enough, a deeply satisfying mélange blue cheese dressing and hot Tabasco sauce add the final touch. A fantastic chicken pizza to enjoy whenever that craving kicks in. Get your own from the Manhattan Pizza Co today!
