Can you feel the love tonight? The most romantic dinners in Singapore for the perfect date

  • 26 September 2017
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 3 min
date night romantic singapore mexican australian cuisine burgers vegan
Share this article

Whether you've finally bagged that date with that hottie in the office or just want a night of romance with your significant other, finding that perfect place is essential for any great date. That's why we've compiled a list of some of the best restaurants in the city to wine and dine the night away with some guilty pleasures.

1.Bel Mondo

It doesn't get more romantic than Italian food, so why not take your special someone along to Bel Mondo for a taste of delicious Italian cuisine? Bel Mondo originated in Osaka, Japan, so there are some Japanese influences in the food which makes it all the most tastier! The menu offers plenty to choose from: pizzas, pastas, risotto - you name it, they've got it. Their lobster pasta is one of the chef's specials, and it does not disappoint. The lobster is enormous and comes with scallops and a huge portion of pasta so you will certainly not be left hungry! Bel Mondo is a great place to impress any date, and who knows, you may be grabbing some food from these guys again to celebrate your anniversary!

Where: Bel Mondo, Raffles Boulevard, Singapore

2.Lucha Loco

For vibrant and delicious Mexican cuisine, Lucha Loco can offer you some of the best Mexican food in town. With vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free options across the board, you don't need to worry about your date not having enough to choose from. There are a range of delicious tacos to try out on this menu. The Chorizo con Res is packed with quality braised beef and herbs which ensure it is brimming with flavour. Order a couple of Mexicani Pilsners to refresh your palate as you eat and you and your date will not be disappointed. Luckily if you feel like staying at home for the date, Lucha Loco offers delivery through Deliveroo, so you can save that cab ride and spend longer cuddled up together.

A post shared by VERONICA PHUA (@veronicaphua) on Sep 12, 2017 at 7:44pm PDT

Where: Lucha Loco, Duxton Hill, Singapore

3.Three Buns by Potato Head Folk

Potato Head Folk is a hipster's paradise in an iconic building filled with eye-catching artistic décor. It doesn't just look pretty, the burgers sold by Three Buns are all anyone has been talking about. Using 100% natural ingredients and the finest artisan bread buns, these burgers offer a mouth-watering meal that will leave your heart and your stomach happy and full. When you're finished eating, grab a couple of drinks to enjoy as the conversations flow. You can even try your hand at making your very own Three Buns Burgers at home and see how yours measure up!

A post shared by Ujiun Yew (@treblebirdo) on Sep 10, 2017 at 2:40am PDT

Where: Three Buns by Potato Head Folk, 36 Keong Saik Road, Singapore

Now you have some of the best venues in the city for the perfect date food with that someone special, so get ordering and have a night to remember! Fancy a romantic night in together? Order your next date night food with Deliveroo!
