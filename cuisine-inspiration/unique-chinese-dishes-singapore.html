Get a kick from these unique Chinese dishes

  • 20 November 2017
  • Swami Sim
  • Read time: 3 min
chinese asian noodles rice seafood trends spicy
Share this article

It can't be argued that Chinese is one of the tastiest cuisines in the world. There's nothing quite like settling down with a hearty Chinese meal for dinner and just tucking in. There are so many unique and exciting Chinese dishes that you probably haven't even heard of that are waiting for you to try them. If you're getting a bit bored of your regular order, spice it up with one of these unique Chinese dishes that will open up your world to new flavours.

1. X.O Sauce Crab Meat Fried Rice

If you're looking for a dish packed full of flavour then look no further. You've heard of classics such as chicken or egg fried rice, so why not try out some crab fried rice? The X.O Sauce Crab Meat Fried Rice served at Lokkee is exactly what it says it is and the X.O sauce adds a tangy twist to the dish adding more layers to the flavour.

Where: Lokkee, Plaza Singapora, Singapore.

A post shared by Kai Koh (@kaikohh) on Nov 13, 2017 at 6:16am PST

2. JUMBO Award-Winning Chilli Crab

JUMBO is known for their great seafood dishes and they never disappoint. If you feel like going for a luxurious and indulgent meal then try out their signature award-winning Chilli Crab. The chilli sauce is a perfect combination of thick, sweet and slightly spicy, but not too much, and complements the crab perfectly. Treat yourself to one of the best chilli crab dishes in Singapore because you know you deserve it.

Where: JUMBO Seafood Gallery, Upper Circular Road, Singapore

A post shared by JUMBO Seafood (@jumboseafoodsg) on Oct 20, 2017 at 2:59am PDT

3. Fish Head Curry Casserole

For some people, the thought of eating the head of something is cringed at and shied away from. However, if you haven't tried out the Fish Head Curry at Ming Kitchen then you are sorely missing out. It is one of their most popular dishes and just one bite will make you understand why. It is served in a delectable casserole and is as tasty as it is hearty and filling.

Where: Ming Kitchen, Tampines, Singapore.

A post shared by emeline joyonegoro 에밀리 (@emelinejoy) on May 4, 2014 at 3:59am PDT

4. Stir-Fried Frog Leg with Dried Chilli

Try something different next time with the Stir-Fried Frogs Leg with Dried Chilli served over at Sing Yi Seafood. There's a reason that the Chinese and the French are known for eating frog's legs in their dishes, they're delicious! Served in a stir-fry with dried chilli sprinkled on top, this dish will surprise you with just how tasty it is and will leave you wondering why you'd never tried it before!

Where: Sing Yi Seafood, Boon Tiong Road, Singapore.

A post shared by Cyn Lim (@xynner) on Sep 20, 2014 at 5:58am PDT

Next time you go for Chinese food, branch out from your usual dish and try something new with any of these deliciously unique Chinese meals. You'll be pleasantly surprised with the new flavours and textures they give you, and you'll be sure to order them time and time again.
