Valentine's Dinner Date? Perfect Romantic Restaurants In Singapore – And What To Order For The Perfect Dinner For Two

  • 6 February 2018
  • Swami Sim
  • Read time: 4 min
valentine's day romantic date singapore risotto sashimi oyster italian ribs
Share this article

It certainly does not have to be Valentine's Day for you to celebrate love in your life – but it doesn't hurt to have an extra excuse to spend some romantic time with your better half! A romantic dinner is one of the best ways to spend your night on the special day, as it gives you a chance to speak and connect as you enjoy your meal together – especially if you are both foodies. From Italian specialities to sumptuous seafood, from American grub to divine desserts, here are plenty of romantic restaurants in Singapore catering to every taste, so you can choose whichever would impress your sweetheart most and make it a memorable night for both.

1. Truly Singaporean Grill

Sometimes love can be messy – especially when you're tucking into a plate of your favourite ribs! In Truly Singaporean Grill they serve them to you St. Louis style, honouring the great American Midwest tradition of choosing only the larger and meatier cuts to cook with. Tender, sticky and falling off the bone, these indulgent pork ribs are slathered in BBQ sauce and are the perfect excuse to get messy with your loved one.

2. The Marmalade Pantry

If you prefer to combine pure deliciousness with something more elegant, head over to The Marmalade Pantry and try out their signature Lobster Barley Risotto. Shiny pearl barley grains are braised with lobster and mixed with crunchy celeriac, onion and carrot bites, all infused with garlic flower and topped with scrumptious lobster tail, fresh prawns and a dollop of sour cream. To cut through that rich taste, finish your meal off with their iconic chocolate pudding – espresso ganache, fresh berries and smooth vanilla custard. Perfect for a romantic dinner for two!

A post shared by The Marmalade Pantry (@themarmaladepantry) on Jan 31, 2018 at 1:24am PST

3. Greenwood Fish Market

Seafood is considered to be on top of the list when it comes to sensual food, and if anyone knows how to do seafood right, it's the people behind Greenwood Fish Market. For an opulent meal, go for their succulent Canadian Golden mantle oyster platter – shellfish as it should be, freshly shucked and with a drizzle of lemon. For something lighter, go for one of their sashimi platters – a luxurious selection of yellow fin tuna, salmon, snapper, king fish and swordfish belly. It's freshly prepared, it's perfect for sharing and it's the most indulgent dish you could wish for on a date.

A post shared by zh89 (@zh89) on Feb 13, 2018 at 3:46am PST

4. Kucina Italian

Romantic and Italian can't help but go hand in hand. A candlelit trattoria, some pasta to share with your amore and ballads playing in the background... you can recreate the scene at home with a little help from Kucina Italian and their truly authentic menu. One of the most romantic restaurants in Singapore, its selection is chock full of pizza and pasta fit to be served in Rome. For a starter, why not share some arancini? This is an authentic Sicilian recipe of saffron infused rice balls coated in breadcrumbs and stuffed with beef ragu, mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Then you could move onto the Florentine classic Bisteca: a mouth-watering black angus rib-eye served the way you like it, with roasted veggies, potato crisps and black pepper sauce. These guys have absolutely nailed decadent romance!
