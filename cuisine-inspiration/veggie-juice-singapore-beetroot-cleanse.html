Carrots, Beetroot And Celery To Go - Veggie Juices To Boost Your Immune System

  • 11 January 2018
  • Vanessa Teo
  • Read time: 4 min
veggie juice healthy vitamins
Share this article

There are some moments in life when we all need a pick-me-up that makes us feel stronger and healthier. This can come in many different ways but few can be tastier and more nutritious than a veggie juice drink. Thankfully, there are some wonderful juice bars and vegetarian takeaway places in Singapore where you can get a juice that is packed with vitamins and other goodness.

1.   Krypto

 

A post shared by Cleanse Bar (@cleansebarsg) on Nov 8, 2015 at 8:54pm PST

At the Cleanse Bar on 1 Maju Ave, you get to choose from an excellent selection of healthy fruit and vegetable drinks. Among their most interesting veggie drinks is the cold-pressed celery, while the Glowing Skin drink brings together carrots and oranges to encourage skin to be smoother and more toned due to high levels of collagen and citric acid. However, if you really want to treat yourself to something that makes you feel great, then the Krypto drink combines the goodness of spinach, romaine, cucumber, parsley, ginger, lemon, and apple to give you an all round nutrient hit.

2.   Cold Pressed Blend: Reboot

A post shared by SaladStop! (@saladstop) on Nov 19, 2017 at 5:29pm PST

You can choose from a tempting range of cold pressed veggie juices at Salad Stop! These include the Reboot drink, which brings together beetroot, apple, honey and carrot for a mixture that is brimming with nutrients such as vitamin C and iron. You will find cucumber, celery and apple in their Alkaline juice but for those with a sweet tooth, there are also a number of fruit juices available as well. Naturally, it is easy to find a great salad here but there are also some tasty but healthy treats such as Blueberry Yoghurt with Granola and Mango Muesli.

3.   ABC Juice with Ginger

A post shared by Fruitee Hut (@fruiteehut) on Jul 5, 2016 at 1:37am PDT

You will find plenty of fabulous juices at the Fruitee Hut, as well as their Fruitee Chips and Soft Served Frozen Yoghurt. In terms of veggie juices that are going to leave you feeling on top of the world, Cucumber Lemon Juice, Carrot Juice and Celery are among the veggie options that might tempt you. An especially interesting item on the menu is their wholesome ABC Juice with Ginger. This is a blend of apple, beetroot, carrot, and ginger, which is thought to reduce muscle pain after that immense workout!

4.   Flu Fighter

A post shared by harvestsg (@harvest.sg) on Nov 16, 2017 at 8:17pm PST

Located on East Coast Road, Harvest is a fine place to add some healthy food and drinks to your life. With a name like Flu Fighter, you can be sure that this veggie drink is exactly what you need whenever you feel that your immune system is in need of a boost. It mixes together watermelon, mint or carrot, apple and orange for a great tasting drink with a lot of valuable properties in it. Another healthy drink well worth trying here is called Liver Elixir. In this case, it is made up of orange, carrot, celery, beetroot, ginger, and lemon.

5.   Vegetapple

The tempting menu at Wild Honey covers a wide range of excellent healthy food and drinks inspired by the US, Europe and elsewhere. For example, you can choose between All Day Breakfasts such as Norwegian, Caribbean, and Scandinavian, as well as a number of different salads. If what you want is a veggie drink that gets you up and running, then you can choose the likes of the lovely Vegetapple. With this juice, you get the stamina increasing benefits of beetroot, carrot, and Granny Smith apples.

Do you feel that it is time to give your immune system a welcome boost? If so, then you can make a great start by looking for the best veggie juices on Deliveroo.
