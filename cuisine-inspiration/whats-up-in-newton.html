Beat the queue in Newton

  • 23 June 2017
  • Timothy Ng
  • Read time: 4 min
newton inspiration noodles tandoori salmon duck chicken rice
Share this article

A good portion of our lives are spent in queues. How much? According to a study done by Prof. Richard Larson at MIT, we spend approximately 2 years of our precious lives in queues. So why wait in line when you can avail your time to do your favourite things? Order from Deliveroo and we will ensure that your time is not wasted.

Newton, A place where residence and offices converge. Surrounded by the famous Newton Hawker Centre, this area has become one of the places to visit. The food options that are within the surrounding are great too! Here are just some of the options to get your taste on!

Boon Tong Kee

Signature Boiled Chicken

Chicken rice. Boon Tong Kee's chicken rice is a testament to uniquely Singapore's national dish. Whole chickens slow poached in stock with garlic, ginger and other spices. This results in a flavorful decedent piece of protein. The rice however, is the true star of the entire dish. Fragrant jasmine rice is cooked with the same stock as the chicken. With the addition of pandan leaves, this dish is a real winner.

For a great plate of a classic taste, order Boon Tong Kee from Deliveroo now!

Collin's Grille Bento

Duck Confit

Our little guilty pleasure. Duck legs slow cooked to perfection in a vat of oil. This results in a plump and crisp duck leg with so much flavor that you might have to order another. Served with mushroom ragout, creamy mashed potatoes and a mesclun salad, this complete meal is worth breaking your diet for.

In Newton and craving for something different? Get some Duck Confit from Collin's Grille Bento on Deliveroo today!

Kazoku Japanese Cuisine

Salmon Don

Fancy a fresh Salmon Don for lunch or dinner? Kazoku Japanese Cuisine is something to check out!

You'd be going back for more! Slices of fresh salmon with fish roe piled on a bed of sushi rice. Best part about this meal is the price. All the above for the price of $8.40. A meal definitely worth your buck.

Crystal Jade Jiangnan

La Mian with Braised Beef Brisket/Minced Meat and Mushroom in Spicy Soup

Hand-Pulled Noodles in a spicy broth is an amazing meal on any given day. Crystal Jade's La Mian is one that is truly astounding. Fresh al dente noodles are par boiled to ensure that the noodles are not over cooked. Topped with your choice of protein (minced meat, mushrooms or braised beef), this meal will warm your soul; and even more so on a cold rainy day.

If you find yourself in Newton, rain or shine, order Crystal Jade Jiangnan from Deliveroo today!

Tandoori Corner

Tandoori Chicken

Great tandoori lamb is something that is extremely hard to find. Sometimes the lamb is too gamey and chewy to enjoy, and sometimes that distinct lamb taste is cooked out of the meat. Tandoori Corner's Tandoori Lamb Chops however are pleasantly spiced and the "essence" of the tandoor still permeates throughout every bite. With its signature spice blend, these Lamb Chops are something worth considering in your next order.

So why wait? Try these great food around your area today! Order from Deliveroo Today!
