What does your favourite breakfast say about you? From early morning eggs to all-day breakfast faves

  • 23 June 2018
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 4 min
breakfast eggs burger bacon avocado acai healthy muffin vegan pancakes
Share this article

It's true what they say about¬†breakfast. Thankfully there are so many options out there these days that you'll be truly spoiled for choice and not inclined to skip the most important meal. Different lifestyles allow for different breakfast options and we've considered them all. Whether you like an¬†indulgent¬†and easy-like-Sunday-morning bite, an all-out all-day breakfast feast or the on-trend healthier alternatives, your breakfast choices can say a lot about you and the day you're about to have!

1. Eggs Benedict - you're a stand up girl/guy

There's really nothing more solid to start your day off right than eggs - so if you're just a solid kind of person, you know that ordering them is getting life right. Eggs benedict originates from New York, a city that never sleeps - so it's a telling choice for someone who's always on the go! Packed full of protein, it'll give you all the energy your day needs and it's absolutely delicious, too. Luckily you don't have to go all the way to the USA to get some: at Little House of Dreams, they're experts on the stuff. Eggs Benedict comes dished up the classic way, served upon a muffin with the poached egg atop some ham. They then add rocket and finish it with hollandaise sauce.

A post shared by Little House of Dreams (@littlehouseofdreams) on Mar 16, 2018 at 8:10pm PDT

2. An acai bowl - you're a bit of a health nut

If you love to start your day the way you mean to go on, an acai bowl is ideal. Health food lovers don't have to sacrifice their way of living - and eating - to get a filling and wholesome brekkie. At An Acai Affair, you could say they know a thing or two about acai (it's all in the name). Their Acai Fruit Bowl comes with acai, fresh fruits, granola, chia seeds, cacao nibs and so much more - it really packs and punch and it'll do you all the way up 'til lunch!

A post shared by An Acai Affair (@anacaiaffair) on May 12, 2018 at 7:38am PDT

3. A breakfast burger - you love a late night

Up late? No problem! Whether you're hungover or just incredibly sleepy, a breakfast burger is a brilliant invention to help you wake up. And, what's more, you can enjoy it all day. If you've woken up late, check out the menu at Miska. Their Breakfast Burger is one of the best - a modern classic, if you will. Burger buns filled with turkey bacon, cheddar cheese, avocado and a fried egg give you everything you didn't know you needed from a breakfast burger - and there are even potato wedges served up on the side!

4. Vegan pancakes - you're cruelty-free but cool

If you're in 2018 and still think a vegan diet is boring, we feel sorry for you! For those who love a good vegan pancake first thing, we know you're definitely on board the "cool vegan" train. Vegan pancakes are not only good for the animals, they're relatively good for you (with a dash of naughtiness as well). At Real Food, they've got that balance down to a tee! Their Sweet Pumpkin Pancakes are brilliant: enjoy organic mashed up pumpkin with soy milk, coconut oil and self-raising flour. It's all combined into tasty pancakes and served with helping after helping of fresh fruit.

No matter what you're looking for, we've got breakfast covered on Deliveroo.
