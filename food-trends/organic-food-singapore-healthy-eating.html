All Aboard the Health Train: Organic Eating in Singapore

  • 25 September 2017
  • Xavier Koh
  • Read time: 4 min
organic healthy food vegan vegetarian singapore
Share this article

It's not always easy to stay healthy, let's face it. Sometimes it's just more convenient to order a pizza for dinner than to cook a healthy meal. But ordering takeout doesn't need to be unhealthy at all. There are more and more restaurants offering wholesome, healthy, organic meals directly to your door.

It's time to look at some of the best places to get your next healthy meal that your body will thank you for.

1.​Veganburg

For when you find yourself craving a burger but feel guilty about the calories, worry no more as Veganburg has you covered. This restaurant serves some of the most delicious burgers in all of Singapore and they are made entirely from vegan produce. These burgers taste so much like real meat that you'll almost be fooled into thinking they are - in the best possible way. One of the most-loved dishes here is the Creamy Shrooms, an entirely plant-based burger made with button mushrooms and dairy-free cream sauce served between wonderfully soft bread buns and will leave your mouth watering for more. Veganburg started in Singapore and is now making a splash in San Francisco, but you don't need to travel far to have a taste, just place your order with Deliveroo and you can have your healthy lunch delivered straight to your door!

A post shared by VeganBurg (@veganburg) on Aug 30, 2017 at 7:03pm PDT

Where: Veganburg, Eunos, Singapore.

2.Vios by Blu Kouzina

Add some excitement to your salads with an authentic Mediterranean twist from Vios by Blu Kouzina. For the perfect healthy lunch bursting with organic goodness, order one of their signature grain bowls which are brimming with wholesome, delicious veggies, grains, dips, proteins and sauces which are so delicious and filling you'll wonder how you ever did without. Like poke bowls, grain bowls are packed with healthy goodness. You can rest assured that you are getting the healthiest meal possible, as the meals at Vios are analysed and crafted by nutritionists to ensure you get as much nourishment for your body as possible. For the perfect post-exercise meal, go for the Power & Recover bowl which is packed with goodies such as meatballs, pan-seared broccoli, sweet potatoes, tzatziki, rice and hummus, designed to restore all that energy and help you make the most of your workout. So, whatever you choose from the menu, you will know that each bowl is made to give your body the healthiest meal possible.

 

A post shared by Cenna (@censkiii) on Aug 15, 2017 at 2:37am PDT

Where: Vios by Blu Kouzina, Tanjong Katong Road, Singapore.

3.Daily Juice

Staying healthy isn't just about the clean eating, but drinking healthy is also important. Daily Juice offers healthy juice and smoothie creations for when you need a nourishing refreshment, or even try a one or three-day juice cleanse. Their juices are cold-pressed and packed with antioxidants and vitamins without the added fructose sugars often found in fruit-only juices. Try out the Krypto juice made with a blend of apple, ginger, romaine, spinach, cucumber, parsley and lemon for a zesty kick which will leave you feeling refreshed and rejuvenated. For a sweeter taste, go for the Apple Pie which is a simple mix of apple and cinnamon to satisfy your sugary cravings.

A post shared by @gninethree on Sep 17, 2015 at 1:23am PDT

Where: Daily Juice, Boat Quay, Singapore.

So, there you go, three places to find some the best organic ingredients in Singapore in some absolutely yummy dishes. No more excuses for eating junk food, as Deliveroo now rewards you for staying healthy by offering prizes when you go for the healthy option on your delivery. If that's not another great reason to stay on the health train, I don't know what is!
