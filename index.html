Full Life

FULL LIFE

From 24 January, customers can give back to the needy and support local farmers through a new 'Farms to Families' initiative by FFTH and The Local Farm by Gardenasia, with just a click of a button via the Deliveroo platform, The food delivery company and its customers have raised $80,000 to-date via an in-app restaurant page, with Deliveroo donating $10,000 of the total amount - supporting more than 1,500 low-income students and families under FFTH's School Goodie Bag programme. Deliveroo will pledge another $10,000 to the latest initiative, supporting local farmers and 2,000 beneficiaries. The initiative is part of Deliveroo's global CSR campaign, Full Life, which aims to utilise its network of riders, restaurants and grocers to support communities globally.

Deliveroo Singapore pledge to devote over 100 hours to improve the lives of over 100 families under Food from the Heart

From October 2021 to April 2022, Deliveroo Singapore will distribute essential food packs to disadvantaged families under Food from the Heart on the last Friday of every month. The initiative is part of Deliveroo's global CSR campaign, Full Life, which aims to utilise its network of riders, restaurants and grocers to support communities globally.
