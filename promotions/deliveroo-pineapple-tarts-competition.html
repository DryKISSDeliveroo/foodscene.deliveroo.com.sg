Think you have the best Pineapple Tarts in town?

  • 16 January 2018
  • Mark Cheong
  • Read time: 8 min
chinese new year pineapple tarts
Share this article

We are on the hunt to find Singapore's best home-baked Pineapple Tarts! The winning baker will win $888 and get a share of the profits when their pineapple tarts are sold exclusively on Deliveroo for Chinese New Year.

Think yours or your auntie's friend's cousin's pineapple tarts are the best in Singapore? Let's find out! We've gathered a panel of judges including some of the best chefs and food connoisseurs to make a decision once and for all.

All you need to do is drop off a sealed box of your pineapple tarts at these locations by 22nd Jan for a chance to win.

AKA Asia, 3 Pickering Street, #01-60 Nankin Row, Singapore 048660 (Weekdays only, 9:00 AM -6:30 PM)

Deliveroo Editions, 332 Tanjong Katong Rd, Singapore 437107 (Weekdays and Weekends, 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM)

Each sealed container must be clearly labelled with your name, email address and phone number.

So what are you waiting for? Let the bake-off commence!
