What’s up in June? This month’s top offers

  • 5 June 2017
  • Mark Cheong
  • Read time: 7 min
1 for 1 free delivery promotions bundles
Share this article

It's June already? Where did May (or April, or March) go? Time sure does fly when you're having awesome food. You've made it this far. Why not celebrate? Here at Deliveroo, we're working with some of our restaurants to make getting this far in the year something worth shouting about.

1-for-1 pizzas

Need we to say more? With Deliveroo, your June mid-year get together might just be adorned with a lovely centerpiece of gourmet pizza for all to share.

The Bakehouse by Carpenter & Cook

We suggest getting their Parma Ham Pizza. A delectable treat with tomatoes, stringy mozzarella cheese, rocket, topped shaved parmesan, and of course, delicious Parma ham. Perhaps the highlight of this pizza is that it's all topped with a generous sprinkling of truffle oil. The truffle fries craze may have come and gone, but with just the right amount of truffle oil, it's got us remembering why we fell in love with it in the first place. With 1-for-1 pizzas at The Bakehouse by Carpenter & Cook, why not pair it up with another of their excellent pizzas? Order it now on Deliveroo.

(Applicable until 30th June)

Free delivery

Here at Deliveroo we're offering free delivery for select restaurants! You won't have to think twice about getting awesome food delivered straight to you.

4Fingers Crispy Chicken

No judgement if you use all 4 fingers on each hand to savour their juicy wings and drumlets. It's in their name, it's in their logo! With their classic Chicken Wings Combo, you get the choice on the flavour of your wings to savour. You decide whether to get it in soy garlic, spicy, or a mix of both. You also get a side of fries that aren't just boring potato flavoured. Have them with either seaweed or kimchi for that extra flavour in your meal. Whichever you choose, be sure to take your time to enjoy the crispy skin, before stripping its juicy chicken meat clean from the bone.

They've also got new Duet, Trio, and Quartet Meals coming soon, so that you and your friends can share a meal!

With new stores at Tiong Bahru, Marina Square, Jurong Point, and Westgate there's even less of a reason not to get your hands dirty. Order 4Fingers Crispy Chicken now from Deliveroo!

Cha Tra Mue

There's never a bad time for Thai Milk Tea. Whether you're out on a hot day, or stuck in the office, Cha Tra Mue's Cold Original Thai Tea is the perfect pick-me-up. We love our tea, and the tea from Cha Tra Mue is as tasty as it gets. The tea used here is fragrant and strong enough to tide you over the afternoon slump, wIth just the right amount of ice so the tea doesn't get too diluted (like we know it can). It's a taste that has a history having been around since 1945, and a taste you would not soon forget. If you're in need your Thai Tea fix, get a Cold Original Thai Tea on Deliveroo.

(Applicable until 30th June)

Limoncello Pizza and Grill

There are days you imagine yourself enjoying a fine traditional pizza on the Amalfi Coast in Italy, looking out into the mediterranean sea. Lucky for you, there's Limoncello Pizza and Grill. Amongst their huge range of sumptuous Italian dishes, we especially love their traditional Margherita Pizza. Made in a traditional Neapolitan style, this simple mix of tomatoes, mozzarella, and fresh basil on an oven baked pizza base makes you feel right at home (in Italy). In this case, less, really is more, as every taste feels perfectly and traditionally balanced. You can now get it with free delivery from Deliveroo, so that mind trip to Italy becomes just that little bit closer to reality. (Applicable until 7th June)

TCC

While a burger can be satisfying, there's something different with TCC's Wagyu Beef Burger. Perhaps it's the juicy tiger prawns topped homemade garlic shrimp floss and zesty citrus-truffle dressing. Perhaps it's the thick sumptuous wagyu beef patty sitting comfortably between 2 black sesame seed buns. Whatever it is, TCC's menu, including their Wagyu Beef Burger is yours to enjoy on Deliveroo, with free delivery.

(Applicable until 30th June)

Bundle sets

Hungry? Ordering for more than just 1? Maybe you've got a huge appetite for meat this June. We've got you covered.

Pasta J

That's where Pasta J comes in. Pasta J have carefully assembled the meats on their menu to form 3 Carnivore Combos. We love their aptly named 'Break-Up Steak', which promises a meal hearty enough to mend broken hearts. You also have a choice on the doneness of the meat. Our personal recommendation will be to have it medium, so you don't lose the juiciness in the meat.
