Bespoke Pizza? Grab a slice!

  • 5 July 2017
  • Mark Cheong
  • Read time: 5 min
pizza pizza party korean italian dessert seafood
Share this article

We're celebrating foodie month in July here at Deliveroo! Kicking it off from 10 - 16 July, we'll be having one big Pizza Party, complete with unique pizzas that you've never seen before, as well as 1-for-1 deals on some of our favourites (but more on that next time).

We're head over heels in love with classics, like pepperoni, margherita, and hawaiian - though the debate on whether pineapples belong on pizza still goes unresolved - but have you ever wanted a pizza that was just a bit different? Enter stage right, our lineup of bespoke pizzas that some of our most creative restaurants have come up with just for the Pizza Party!

Da Paolo PizzaBar

Spaghetti and Meatball Pizza

Da Paolo PizzaBar's pizzas are gourmet wonders. These are people who have a firm understanding that pizza is meant to be enjoyed, and it all stems from having a good pizza to start off with. Oven baked with a traditional thin crust, each pizza is a joy to share, and in some cases, have all by yourself.

Their special Spaghetti and Meatball Pizza is a radical spin on the traditional fare we're used to seeing. If you have an inkling towards Italian food but can't decide between pasta and pizza, the two pinnacles of italian cuisine, the Spaghetti and Meatball Pizza is the answer. You wouldn't expect these two to go so well together in one bite but they do. It combines the joy of biting into a slice with the silky smooth texture of spaghetti, topped with juicy meatballs, parmesan cheese, and smothered in Italian tomato sauce. Top it all off with parsley and fresh basil to complete the ultimate fusion of Italian food.

Have a slice of Da Paolo PizzaBar's Spaghetti and Meatball Pizza, only on Deliveroo.

Meat Smith

Meat Smith Meat Lovers

We love to have meat on our pizzas. The meatier, the better. That's why we've got together with Meat Smith to create the Meat Smith Meat Lovers Pizza. These guys love and know their meat, so you know that a pizza from them is in good hands. With the Meat Smith Meat Lovers Pizza, we've got our favourite meat platter put on a pizza base in our favourite and familiar shape. Never has a slice of pizza been so overflowing with juicy red meat, each bite a hearty treat.

Get the Meat Smith Meat Lovers Pizza on Deliveroo during our Pizza Party week!

Pasta Fresca Da Salvatore

Pizza Gamberi e Zucchine

The Pizza Gamberi e Zucchine from Pasta Fresca Da Salvatore is a twist on our favourite seafood pizzas. We're big fans of the white pizza base with creamy mozzarella cheese. As part of the seafood fare, the pizza is topped with prawns, as well as crunchy zucchini slices, juicy cherry tomatoes, with a sprinkling of parsley and garlic oil. The result is a creamy and juicy pizza, that anyone who loves their pizza white, would love.

Get the Pizza Gamberi e Zucchine from Pasta Fresca Da Salvatore, only on Deliveroo.

Seoul Kitchen

Korean Chicken Jeon Pizza

Here's a big twist on the pizza we know and love. The K-wave has hit Italian cuisine, and Seoul Kitchen have come up with the Korean Chicken Jeon Pizza. This creation fuses some of the most iconic Korean tastes, with our favourite Italian slice of life, bringing us something uniquely cross-cultural. The pan is slathered with a simple but generous serving of cheese, chicken and mushroom, all doused in their special Korean sauce.

Get the Korean Chicken Jeon Pizza on Seoul Kitchen, only on Deliveroo.

The Grumpy Bear

Waffle Pizza

Have you ever looked at other circular foods and thought "wait a minute… that's a pizza too! Amazing!" If you're open minded enough, any kind of food can be made into, or at least imagined as a pizza. When you're craving for dessert, but also a pizza (read: all the time), why not try Waffle Pizza from The Grumpy Bear? Just imagine the fluffy goodness of a waffle, topped with marshmallows, cherry, nuts, and drizzled with chocolate and sweet maple sauce, but have it cut into the familiar shape of a pizza slice for maximum enjoyment.

Get unique pizzas only on Deliveroo from 10th - 16th July!
