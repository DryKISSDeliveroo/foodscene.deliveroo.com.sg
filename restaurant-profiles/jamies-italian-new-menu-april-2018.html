Jamie’s Italian has a new menu (and it’s good)

  • 17 April 2018
  • Mark Cheong
  • Read time: 3 min
jamie's italian italian pasta pizza salad
Share this article

It's true. Jamie's Italian's new seasonal lineup of Italian classics with a Jamie Oliver's twist is now up and available on Deliveroo. These aren't just delicious dishes, these are finely crafted masterpieces made with the finest and freshest gourmet ingredients. Lucky for us, we got to have a look and try it out for ourselves too.

Jamie's Big Five Salad

We're starting off with salads, with Jamie Oliver being a big advocate of eating well. Jamie's Big Five Salad is exactly this - five fruits and vegetables in the meal - in a refreshing bowl of quinoa, fennel, peas, crunchy red chicory, grapes, radish, and plenty of herbs, tossed with lemony extra virgin olive oil. If you're feeling extra hungry, add smoked salmon, proscuitto, or grilled chicken! Get it from Jamie's Italian.

Chicken Casarecce

We know Jamie's Italian for their delicious pastas, handmade fresh in-house every day. Good news for us, they've added to the lineup with a new selection of pastas tossed in flavourful sauces and the freshest gourmet ingredients. Tuck into a comforting bowl of Chicken Casarecce - homemade twisted and rolled pasta served with braised chicken thigh in a creamy sauce with bacon, leeks, roasted mushrooms and herbs. Exclusively available at Jamie's Italian - Vivo City.

The Porkie

The Porkie is a cheeky meat laden pizza that will have any meat lover excited. Grab a slice of this crisp, thin crust pizza topped with sweet tomato sauce, hot soppressata, smoky bacon, mozzarella and oregano, only at Jamie's Italian - Vivo City. 

There's more than just these three new additions to the team, so check out Jamie's Italian on Deliveroo to see what else is available!
